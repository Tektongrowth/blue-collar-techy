The Point

Your marketing agency is not the hero. You are.

I've been on both sides of this. I hired agencies as a contractor. Most of them overpromised and underdelivered. So I taught myself, got better results, and eventually made it my career.

Marketing plays a real part. But the business owner still owns pieces of the pie no agency can touch. Your reputation. Your follow-up. How you show up on the jobsite.

Blue Collar Techy is for contractors who understand that.
