# GoHighLevel Walkthrough Video Series — Plan

**Goal:** Replace the old "spreadsheet lead magnet" plan with a YouTube + blog series that shows contractors how to actually run their business in a real CRM. Every video is a concrete "build this in GHL" walkthrough, paired with a companion blog post and a screen-recording on YouTube (@nicklausconley).

**Why this and not a PDF or template pack:**
Blue Collar Techy's whole positioning is "practical tech for the trades." A video series that actually shows the tech working outperforms any static resource — and it's the format Nick has real leverage in (10 years in the trades + an agency that runs on GHL daily).

**Production pattern:**
- Each video is 8-15 minutes, screen-recorded in actual GHL with a real contractor-style test account
- Companion blog post on bluecollartechy.com with screenshots + step list (not a transcript, a referenceable doc)
- The blog post embeds the YouTube video at the top
- All videos link to each other as a series, and point to /work-with-me for "want us to build this for you"
- Affiliate link to GHL in every description (per global affiliate-link rule)

## Series outline (7 videos)

### 1. Review automation (paired with post 1 "how-to-get-more-google-reviews")
**Promise:** Build the three-moment review ask into GHL so it runs itself on every project.
**Covers:**
- Pipeline stages: Contract Signed, Mid-Project, Job Complete
- Trigger 1: day-one expectation email (fires on Contract Signed)
- Trigger 2: milestone check-in SMS (fires when you manually move pipeline to Mid-Project)
- Trigger 3: walkthrough ask email + same-day SMS (fires on Job Complete)
- Trigger 4: day-3 "one quick favor" fallback email
- Review link shortener setup
- How to see who did/didn't leave a review in the opportunities view

### 2. Pipeline setup from scratch (paired with post 3 "best-lead-generation-companies-for-contractors")
**Promise:** A working GHL pipeline a contractor can clone in under 30 minutes.
**Covers:**
- Stages: Lead In, Contacted, Estimate Sent, Won, Lost, Ghosted
- Custom fields: project type, budget, neighborhood, lead source, estimate amount
- Stage-based automations: 5-minute response SMS, 24-hour estimate follow-up, 7-day "still interested" text
- Won/Lost reasons — the tracking that tells you what you're actually losing on
- Reports view so the weekly number is one click away

### 3. Missed-call text-back (paired with post 8 "best-contractor-websites")
**Promise:** Never lose a lead to a missed call again.
**Covers:**
- GHL phone number setup (or port existing)
- Missed-call trigger → auto SMS within 60 seconds
- Personalized SMS copy that doesn't sound like a bot
- Callback scheduling link embedded in the SMS
- How to route after-hours calls differently

### 4. Past-customer reactivation (paired with post 6 "how-to-get-leads-as-a-contractor")
**Promise:** Turn your 100-job backlog into 5-10 new jobs this quarter.
**Covers:**
- Filter contacts by last job date (90+ days ago)
- Segment by project type
- A 3-touch reactivation sequence (seasonal offer, maintenance check, referral ask)
- How to not burn the list (pace it, skip recent customers, drop people who unsubscribe)

### 5. Local Service Ads lead intake (paired with post 4 "local-service-ads-for-contractors")
**Promise:** Every LSA lead hits your pipeline and gets a response in under 60 seconds.
**Covers:**
- LSA webhook → GHL contact creation
- Auto-tag by service type
- 60-second response SMS trigger
- Appointment booking link in the first reply
- LSA-specific pipeline stage tracking so you can see CPL by source

### 6. Referral ask automation (no specific post — standalone)
**Promise:** Make the referral ask happen every time without remembering to ask.
**Covers:**
- Trigger: job marked complete + review left (5-star only)
- 7-day post-review SMS asking for one referral
- Referral landing page with short form
- Auto-tag on referrer contact + referral contact
- Dashboard view of referrals per contact

### 7. Monthly report automation (standalone)
**Promise:** You stop asking "how many leads did we get this month?"
**Covers:**
- Dashboard widgets: leads by source, conversion rate, won revenue
- Monthly email report to the owner
- Quarterly comparison view
- Where the numbers lie and what to watch instead
